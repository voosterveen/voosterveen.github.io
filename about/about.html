About

During my bachelor in Sociology I discovered my interest for methods and statistics. That is why I started the master programme Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences. Within the programme there is the opportunity to enroll in the European Master in Official Statistics, which I did. Currently, I am writing my thesis at Statistics Netherlands (in Dutch: Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek).

Next to studying, I am working freelance as a statistics tutor. I help all kinds of students: bachelor to PhD, veterinary sciences to behavioural sciences. For a lot of students, elaborate courses on statistics or working with SPSS or R are lacking. But they are expected to know how to conduct statistical analysis for their thesis! My goal is to show students that they are able to understand and apply statistics.
